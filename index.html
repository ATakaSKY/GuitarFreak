Types

Acoustic

Has gentle rhythmic chordal strumming. Suitable for a beginner.

Electric

Solid-bodied guitars designed to be plugged into an amplifier.

Twelve-string

Has six regular strings and a second set of thinner strings.

Steel

Originated from Hawaiin. Played horizontally across the player's lap.

Bass

Has a long neck and thick strings. The standard bass has four strings though five and six string basses are available which extends the range of the instrument.

Double Neck

Designed so that two guitar necks can share one body. This design allows the guitarist to switch between either neck with ease.

Legends

Angus Young

Angus McKinnon Young (born 31 March 1955) is an Australian guitarist, best known as the co-founder, lead guitarist, songwriter and sole constant member of the Australian hard rock band AC/DC.
Young was ranked 24th in Rolling Stone magazine's 100 greatest guitarist of all-time list.

Matthew Tuck

Matthew "Matt" Tuck (born 20 January 1980) is a Welsh musician. He is the lead vocalist and rhythm guitarist in the Welsh heavy metal band Bullet for My Valentine. He is also a singer and guitarist in the supergroup AxeWound, which was formed in 2012. He also plays piano, drums, keyboard, and harmonica.

Kirk Hammett

Kirk Lee Hammett (born November 18, 1962) is the lead guitarist and contributing songwriter for the heavy metal band Metallica and has been a member of the band since 1983. In 2009, Hammett was ranked number 15 in Joel McIver's book The 100 Greatest Metal Guitarists.
